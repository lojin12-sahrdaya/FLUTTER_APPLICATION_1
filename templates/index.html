About Gesture Space

App Name

Gesture Space v1.0

Purpose

Gesture Space is a hands-free desktop control application that lets you interact with your computer using simple hand gestures and eye movement. It is designed for convenience, accessibility, and touchless interaction.

Help & Gestures Guide

Hand Gestures

  • Move Cursor
    Move the palm
  • Right Click
    Press Middle finger + Thumb
  • Left Click
    Press Index finger + Thumb
  • Double Click
    Press Ring finger + Thumb
  • Scroll Up/Down
    Make a fist & move hand up/down
  • Volume Up/Down
    Press Pinky finger + Thumb & move up/down

Head Tracking & Voice Control

  • Move Cursor
    Move your head
  • Voice Commands
    • Right Click / Left Click / Double Click
    • Drag Start / Drag End
    • Scroll Up / Scroll Down
    • VU / VD (Volume Up/Down)
    • BU / BD (Brightness Up/Down)
    • Noise Low / Medium / High
